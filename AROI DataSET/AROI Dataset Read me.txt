AROI DATASET
===============
We have collected the publicly available AROIdataset by email communication.

This dataset belongs Wet AMD volume data. Here we keep the data in WetAMD folder. The dry and Non AMD folders are empty. The used 33 fovea centric extracted denoised slices of AROI dataset link is: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1aK2if6UH3KSjaOmGzJjFs8MbamYfnTgQ?usp=sharing

M. Melinščak, M. Radmilović, Z. Vatavuk, and S. Lončarić, "AROI: Annotated Retinal OCT Images database," in 2021 44th International Convention on Information, Communication and Electronic Technology (MIPRO), Sep. 2021, pp. 400-405.
